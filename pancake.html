Pancake

Description

Whether it's breakfast or brunch, fluffy pancakes are always a hit! This easy recipe guarantees golden, soft pancakes that melt in your mouth. Perfect for a cozy morning or a sweet treat during the day. Let’s whip up some happiness on a plate!

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 ¼ cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter
  • Butter or oil for cooking

Steps

  1. In a bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt.
  2. Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg, and melted butter. Mix until smooth.
  3. Heat a lightly oiled frying pan over medium-high heat.
  4. Pour about 1/4 cup of batter for each pancake into the pan.
  5. Brown on both sides and serve hot with your favorite toppings — like maple syrup, fruit, or chocolate.
